Privacy Policy

Google Analytics

Like millions of other websites, I make use of Google Analytics to help analyze how our visitors use this site. Google Analytics uses “cookies” to collect standard website log information about visitors. All information collected is anonymous; it’s basically a help to better understand the kinds of visitors the site receives. I fully abide by Google’s Terms of Service – including having this privacy policy.

want to opt out?

You can opt out of tracking, not just for this website but all sites that use Google Analytics, by disabling JavaScript with NoScript, or alternatively using Google’s own browser extension.

other information collected

If you submit a blog comment, I request your name and email address, and optionally your website. I do this firstly to ensure you are not a robot or spammer, and secondly so that your comments can be attributed to you. If you wish your personal information to be removed from this site, simply contact me at and I will do so at the earliest opportunity. Note that in removing your personal details all of the blog comments you have made will also be deleted.

comments or concerns

If you have any comments or concerns about this policy or the website in general, don’t hesisate to contact me.
